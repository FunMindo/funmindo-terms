Leaderboard & Player Name System

Stack It! offers optional global leaderboards (Weekly and All-Time) powered by Unity Gaming Services. Participation in leaderboards is entirely voluntary.

How the Name System Works

Stack It! uses a safe, predefined name system specifically designed for our 3+ audience. Here is how it works:

  • When you access the leaderboard for the first time, a name selection screen will appear
  • You are presented with a randomly generated name chosen from a curated list of safe, age-appropriate predefined names
  • You may shuffle the name as many times as you like until you find one you are happy with
  • Once you tap Save, your name is locked in permanently — it cannot be changed after saving
  • Your saved name will then appear next to your score on the leaderboard
🛡️ Child-safe by design. Because all names are predefined and curated by FunMindo, no player (including young children) can enter offensive, personal, or inappropriate names. This system protects all players on the leaderboard.

Leaderboard Integrity

  • Only scores achieved through normal, legitimate gameplay will be accepted
  • Scores generated via cheats, hacks, modified APKs, or exploits will be removed
  • FunMindo reserves the right to remove any leaderboard entry it deems suspicious or in violation of these Terms, without prior notice
  • FunMindo does not guarantee leaderboard availability at all times — the service may be temporarily unavailable due to maintenance or technical issues
  • Weekly leaderboards reset on a regular schedule; historical weekly scores may not be retained

To request removal of your leaderboard entry and associated player data, contact us at funmindostudio@gmail.com.
